ABOUT US

JP Transportation Safety Consulting, LLC (JPTSC) is located in Birmingham, Alabama specializing in consulting services for private and for-hire trucking companies operating commercial motor vehicles in intrastate and interstate commerce.

Our goal is to provide our clients with consulting services that will enable motor carriers to develop the necessary safety management controls to further improve company safety fitness standards and reduce vehicle accidents and injuries. JPTSC is designed to give attorneys and trucking companies a more in depth understanding of required federal and state regulations pertaining to safe commercial motor vehicle operations.

JPTSC is a member of the North American Transportation Management Institute (NATMI), Alabama Trucking Association (ATA), and the National Safety Council (NSC).
